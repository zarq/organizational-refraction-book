## 1. Availability / offer

**Headline:** Keynotes, workshops, and board sessions.

The author works directly with executive teams on the problem the book diagnoses: finding where strategic intent bends in *their* organization, and correcting it. Sessions are built for senior audiences who are past inspiration and want a structural method they can apply on Monday.

**Formats:**
- **Keynote (45–60 min).** The refraction model and why the strategy-reality gap persists — reframing the room's hardest execution problem as a transmission problem. Ideal for leadership summits and offsites.
- **Leadership workshop (half- or full-day).** A working session in which your team maps its own refractive profile — the boundaries where intent is bending hardest — and leaves with a prioritized correction list.
- **Board / executive session (90 min).** A focused diagnostic for boards and C-suites weighing why a major strategy isn't landing, and what to do instead of pushing harder.
